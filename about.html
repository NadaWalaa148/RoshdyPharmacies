We are Roshdy
Pharmacies

Roshdy Pharmacies opened its first branch in 2008, since then Roshdy has continually employed the best practices and techniques at its disposal to improve our brand and earn the trust of our customers. It is upon this trust that Roshdy has built its reputation and its success.

Overview

We strive to continually earn the trust of our customers. We want our customers to have the most confidence in our products, services and advices. This trust is the foundation of everything we do. This trust is the foundation of everything we do.

Our Values

.Respect Treat everyone in our diverse community, including patients, their families and colleagues, with dignity. Customer must be respected and always be informed.
.Caring Provide the best care, treating our customers and family members with sensitivity and empathy.
.Integrity Adhere to the highest standards of professionalism, ethics and personal responsibility, worthy of the trust our patients place in us.
.Service We strive to exceed our patients’ and/or fellow caregivers’ expectations for comfort and convenience.
.Teamwork Value the contributions of all, blending the skills of individual staff members in unsurpassed collaboration.
.Excellence Deliver the best outcomes and highest quality service through the dedicated effort of every team member.
.Innovation Infuse and energize the organization, enhancing the lives of those we serve, through the creative ideas and unique talents of each employee.

Our Mission

We are a pharmacies' chain offering professional services and wide varieties of health and beauty products through professional trustable team targeting operational excellence to satisfy our customers.

Our Vision

To be the market leading pharmacies' chain providing sustainable developing health & beauty services and products to build better future for our communities.
